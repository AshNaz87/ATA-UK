Our History

Afghanistan-Turks Association (ATA-UK) is a non-government and not-for-profit community organisation established in November 2013 in Cambridge, United Kingdom (Pending registration). ATA seeks to bring together, engage, integrate, help and support Afghan-Turks diaspora in community-led activities across the United Kingdom.


ATA-UK envisions to creating a cohesive community, facilitate and organise cultural activities and practices, indoor games and entertainment for young people, provide training, advocacy, advice to community members, particularly to the needy, elderly and refugees.
