Our History

Afghanistan-Turks Association (ATA-UK) is a non-government and not-for-profit community organisation founded in November 2013 in Cambridge, United Kingdom. We seek to bring together, engage with, help integrate, and support the Afghan-Turkic diaspora in the UK through community-led activities.

In espousing a cohesive community, we have and aim to facilitate and organise cultural activities and practices, organise indoor games and entertainment for the youth and to provide training, advocacy, advice to community members, particularly to the needy, elderly and refugees.
