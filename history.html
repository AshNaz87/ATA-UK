Our History

Afghanistan-Turks Association (ATA-UK) is a non-government and not-for-profit community organisation established in November 2013 in Cambridge, United Kingdom. ATA-UK seeks to bring together, engage with, integrate, help and support the Afghan-Turkic diaspora through community-led activities across the United Kingdom.


ATA-UK envisions in creating a cohesive community, to facilitate and organise cultural activities and practices, organise indoor games and entertainment for the youth and to provide training, advocacy, advice to community members, particularly to the needy, elderly and refugees.
